a_POSCAR_primitivecell is the structure file of the primitive cell;
a_POSCAR_supercell is the structure file of the supercell;
c_abs(jdos).dat is absorption spectrum contributed by different direct e-h pairs and the JDOS of indirect e-h pairs;
f_coff4dk.txt is the k-points coordinates of the primitive cell folded to the supercell k-points;
f_gw_wannier90_band.dat is the same as a_gw_wannier90_band.dat in Fig.1;
